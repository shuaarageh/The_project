Treadmill

It’s basically a piece of machine designed for running in the same space via the use of a rotating belt.
This actually dates back centuries to standard mills, which were manually operated by animals or humans

There are plenty of treadmills available even for home-gym use that are quite affordable.

Dumbbells

ــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــ

Dumbbells are possibly the most known free weights in the world.
They are used in weight training either individually, or in pairs.
The handle is usually made of iron or steel and
the weight parts are either made of iron, steel, cement or rubber.
These dumbbells can also be adjustable
meaning you can change the desired weight on each dumbbell by
unscrewing and re-screwing the weight plates from the handle.





Resistance Bands

ــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــ

Resistance bands are basically stretchy
pieces of plastic that use different weights of resistance.
Resistance training increases one’s strength, for example by using them for bicep curls.







Stability Ball

ــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــــ

Also called exercise ball or Swiss ball is a huge and versatile piece of fitness equipment that
can be used for various exercise for the entire body.
It is a great tool to increase one’s stability, overall balance and core strength.
It’s funny that you can even lose calories with a stability ball simply by
bouncing up and down on it like a crazy person.
